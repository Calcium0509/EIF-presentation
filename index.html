EdChats action steps

To achieve a goal in web development, a Year 10 student should start by defining their goal using the SMART criteria: specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound. Research supports this approach for enhancing focus and motivation. They should then identify resources like online courses and set milestones to break the goal into manageable tasks, as studies show this improves learning. Developing skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript is crucial, with practice through small projects. Seeking feedback from online communities can provide valuable insights. Regular reflection and adjustment of plans will help them stay on track. Staying motivated by celebrating achievements and exploring new technologies will maintain enthusiasm. By following these steps, they can create a purposeful path to success in web development.
